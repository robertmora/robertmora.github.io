Teaching is one of my favourite parts of the job. Most of my courses fall under the broad umbrella of networked systems, but in reality they mix a bit of everything I care about: protocols, distributed systems, service provisioning over networks, standards, and how all these pieces behave once deployed in the wild. I try to keep classes practical, system-oriented, and grounded in real-world deployments. Below is an overview of the courses I currently teach and my past teaching activities. You can read more about my teaching philosophy here. (link coming soon!)
